🐾 User Guide:
🐾 Starting the Analysis: Upon launching the application, you'll be greeted with options for the two types of analysis. Simply click on the one you wish to use.
  From Theme selection you can select the themes like light mode, dark mode or system default.

🐾 Conducting Cognitive Dysfunction Prediction:
  Fill in twelve queries about your dog, including manual entry of weight. All questions must be completed to proceed with the analysis.
  Experience the luxury of analysis result with a simple click of the 'Submit' button.
  Depending on the initial results, you may be asked to answer six additional queries after a six-month period for enhanced accuracy. 
  Upon clickingr the 'Submit Follow-up' option, you will receive a detailed and sophisticated cognitive dysfunction score.

🐾 Performing Regression Analysis:
  Choose a disease from the provided list of nine.
  Select one or more variables to associate with the chosen disease.
  Click on 'Get data for the desired choices' and wait a few seconds for the results to load.
